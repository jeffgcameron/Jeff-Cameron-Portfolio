About Me

Hi my name is Jeff and I am a web developer living in Jackson, Wy. I am currently attending The University of Denver's full stack coding bootcamp, focusing on HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I graduated from Elon University in 2016 with a Bachelors Degree in Business Management and a minor in Asian Studies. During the fall semester of my junior year, I studied abroad in Beijing where I fell in love with traveling and exploring. Since then, I have seized every opportunity to travel and explore both abroad and domestically. When I am not coding, working, or taveling, I can be found exploring Jackson's surrounding mountains and rivers. My favorite hobbies include skiing, mountain biking, surfing, scuba diving, rafting, rollerblading and fly fishing. My excitement for new andexciting challeges led me to coding and web development. The fusion of creativity and logic makes coding both fun and interesting. I am excited to see where my new skills take me!
